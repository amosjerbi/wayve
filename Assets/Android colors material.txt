Primary Colors
primary - Main brand color, used for primary actions
onPrimary - Text/icons on primary color
primaryContainer - Less prominent than primary
onPrimaryContainer - Text/icons on primaryContainer

Secondary Colors
secondary - Secondary brand color, used for less prominent actions
onSecondary - Text/icons on secondary color
secondaryContainer - Less prominent than secondary (currently: light blue for focused fields)
onSecondaryContainer - Text/icons on secondaryContainer


Tertiary Colors
tertiary - Tertiary brand color, used for contrasting accents
onTertiary - Text/icons on tertiary color
tertiaryContainer - Less prominent than tertiary (currently: used for unfocused fields)
onTertiaryContainer - Text/icons on tertiaryContainer

Error Colors
error - Error states and alerts
onError - Text/icons on error color
errorContainer - Less prominent error backgrounds
onErrorContainer - Text/icons on errorContainer

Background & Surface
background - Main app background
onBackground - Text/icons on background
surface - Component surfaces (cards, sheets, menus)
onSurface - Text/icons on surface
surfaceVariant - Alternative surface color with less emphasis
onSurfaceVariant - Text/icons on surfaceVariant
surfaceTint - Tint color for elevated surfaces
surfaceBright - Brighter surface variant
surfaceDim - Dimmer surface variant
surfaceContainer - Container surfaces (currently: used for cards)
surfaceContainerHigh - Higher emphasis container
surfaceContainerHighest - Highest emphasis container
surfaceContainerHigh - Higher emphasis container
surfaceContainerHighest - Highest emphasis container
surfaceContainerLow - Lower emphasis container
surfaceContainerLowest - Lowest emphasis container

Inverse Colors
inverseSurface - Inverted surface color (for tooltips, snackbars)
inverseOnSurface - Text/icons on inverseSurface
inversePrimary - Inverted primary color

Outline & Scrim
outline - Border and divider lines
outlineVariant - Lower emphasis borders
scrim - Overlay for modals/dialogs
